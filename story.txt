This is the story of our first collaboration. A young cartographer named Eli discovers an old map tucked inside a library book — one that shows a mountain that doesn't exist on any modern chart. He sets out alone into the northern wilderness, compass in hand, certain the map is a hoax. But on the third day, the trees thin out, and there it is: a peak wreathed in mist, and at its base, a door carved into the rock.
